About us

Private credit is huge, opaque, and mostly unreadable. We're fixing the readable part.

Credit Canon exists to make the private credit universe transparent, for every investor, allocator, regulator, and journalist trying to understand it.

Private credit has become one of the biggest, murkiest corners of finance. The numbers exist, but they are scattered across hundreds of SEC filings, in formats that fight you, behind terminals most people cannot afford. We put them in one place. Every figure traces back to a primary filing, so you can see what is actually happening. No Bloomberg subscription. No taking anyone's word for it.

What makes us different

The Stress Desk Premium

Anyone can show you what the market looks like today. Our edge is showing you what it looks like under pressure. The Stress Desk lets you stress-test the entire private credit market, raise defaults, widen spreads, freeze redemptions, and watch how every fund holds up. It's the one paid part of Credit Canon, and it's the reason the rest can stay open.

Where we're headed

Today, Credit Canon covers business development companies, the most filing-transparent slice of private credit. But BDCs are just the start. The plan is to bring the same readability to the whole private credit universe.
