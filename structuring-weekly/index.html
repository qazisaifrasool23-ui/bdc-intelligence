What this is

Structuring Weekly is the writing arm of BDC Intelligence. The platform tells you what the numbers are. The publication tells you what they mean, why they got there, and what to look for next.

Every issue is something a credit analyst could read on the train and walk away smarter. No filler. No jargon left unexplained. No takes without the structure underneath them.

Who writes it

Qazi Rasool and Sam Soukas, co founders of BDC Intelligence. One came from the deal side. The other spent his undergrad inside the buy side. Every piece is one of us writing lead with the other reviewing, so the same standard hits the page every week.
